Who is behind ImpoFinds - Tinapay's Anime Collections?

Tinapay’s Anime Collection, managed by Cristina Grace J. Cantilan—one of the owners of ImpoFinds—is a specialized division of the business that focuses on selling branded, authentic, and high-quality anime merchandise sourced directly from Japan. While ImpoFinds caters to a wide range of anime-themed products, Tinapay’s Anime Collection highlights exclusive Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba merchandise, including Japanese and English mangas, collectible figurines, keychains, plushies, and other related items.

This focused collection ensures that dedicated fans and collectors of Demon Slayer have access to carefully curated and genuine products, whether for personal enjoyment or investment purposes.

Why ImpoFinds?

An important aspect of our business is investment driven growth. Rather than relying solely on internal capital, we seek investors who can contribute to the expansion of our inventory and market reach and giving them an option to cycle their money back into inventory for a higher buy out later due to their reinvestment or they get the profits of their investment in return.

We specifically seek investors to support inventory growth rather than the physical aspects of business operations. Their contributions will directly enhance our product offerings and market reach, ultimately leading to higher profit yields.
